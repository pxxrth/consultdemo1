Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026  |  Applies to: lumen-pk.com

1. Who We Are

Lumen ("we," "us," "our") is a sole proprietorship operating under the name Lumen, registered in Pakistan. We provide informational consultation services related to dermatology and aesthetic care, conducted by licensed dermatologists and aesthetic physicians, and refer clients to partner clinics for full clinical assessment and treatment.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our services, we may collect the following:

  • Contact details – name, phone number, email address
  • Consultation details – skin concerns or aesthetic queries you voluntarily share with us
  • Payment information – processed securely through third-party payment processors (EasyPaisa, JazzCash, HBL). We do not store your card or wallet credentials.
  • Device and usage data – IP address, browser type, pages visited (collected automatically via standard web analytics)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To match you with an appropriate partner clinic
  • To communicate your consultation summary to the partner clinic you are referred to
  • To send you appointment reminders or follow-up messages via WhatsApp or email
  • To process your payment
  • To improve our service

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share relevant details with:

  • Partner clinics – only the information necessary to facilitate your referral and appointment
  • Payment processors – to complete your transaction securely
  • Legal authorities – if required by Pakistani law or regulatory order

Partner clinics operate independently and have their own privacy and data practices. We encourage you to review their policies before attending an appointment.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Google Workspace tools (Forms, Sheets) and is accessible only to authorised Lumen personnel. We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorised access. However, no digital system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute data security.

6. Retention

We retain your personal information for up to 12 months from your last interaction with us, after which it is deleted or anonymised unless legal obligations require otherwise.

7. Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time by contacting us at the details below. We will respond within 7 business days.

8. Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies to improve your browsing experience. No tracking cookies from third-party advertisers are used. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

9. Children

Our services are not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors without parental consent.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: April 2026  |  Applies to: lumen-pk.com
Please read this policy carefully before making a payment. By completing a booking with Lumen, you confirm that you have read and agreed to these terms.

1. What You Are Paying For

Lumen charges a consultation fee for the service of reviewing your submitted skin or aesthetic concern, providing a general informational summary, and referring you to a suitable partner clinic. This is a service fee — not a medical fee — and does not include any clinical examination, diagnosis, prescription, or treatment.

2. Eligibility for Refund

You are entitled to a full refund if:

  • You cancel your consultation request before a consultant has reviewed your submission (typically within 2 hours of payment)
  • We are unable to match you with a suitable partner clinic for your concern
  • You did not receive any service response from us within 48 hours of your confirmed booking

3. No Refund Circumstances

Refunds will not be issued if:

  • A consultation summary has already been prepared and delivered to you
  • You have been successfully referred to a partner clinic
  • You are dissatisfied with the outcome of your visit to the partner clinic — the partner clinic's services are entirely separate from Lumen's service
  • You provided incomplete or inaccurate information that affected the consultation

4. Partial Refunds

At our discretion, we may issue a partial refund in cases where a consultation was initiated but not completed due to circumstances on our end. This will be assessed on a case-by-case basis.

5. No Returns

As Lumen provides a service rather than a physical product, there is no return process applicable. The concept of "returns" does not apply to our business model.

6. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact us within 48 hours of your payment:

  • Email: support@lumen-pk.com
  • Subject line: "Refund Request – [Your Name] – [Date of Booking]"

Please include your payment reference number and reason for the request. Approved refunds will be processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

7. Cancellation by Lumen

We reserve the right to cancel and fully refund a booking if we determine we are unable to serve your request appropriately, or if the nature of the concern requires immediate emergency medical attention — in which case we will direct you to seek urgent care.

8. Medical Disclaimer

Lumen is not a medical provider. Refund disputes relating to clinical outcomes, misdiagnosis, or treatment received at a partner clinic are outside the scope of this policy and should be directed to the relevant clinic. Lumen accepts no liability for clinical decisions made by partner clinics.

For refund or cancellation queries: support@lumen-pk.com  |  lumen-pk.com
